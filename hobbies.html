Although I have been working with clay for years, in my sophmore year of university I got the opportunity to participate in my university pottery co-op as a co-head manager. Although the studio has been closed for covid, I cannot wait to rejoin the co-op as it opens and continue to create amazing projects.

I love making all sorts of functional sculptures, from water fountains to planters to even a lamp! Join me for an adventure of creation as I continue to explore the boundries of clay.
